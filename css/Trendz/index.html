About us

Trendz Import Export is a certified India based Importer and Exporter. We are dealing in many fabric products and customise our products as per customer needs and demands. We are specialized in sourcing the following products, from our highly skilled sourcing partners, such as Texile Fabrics, Woven, Denim, Man Made Fiber, Yarn etc.

We are currently exporting to Peru & Bolivia (South America Countries) . Within very short time span, we have successfully supplied high quality manufactured fabrics, making them our satisfied clients. We are also in process of associating with Thailand Companies for multiple products for Exporting and Importing to other Country Locations.

We co-ordinate our efforts with a skilled, talented, experienced team of industry experts for each step of the export process to provide the most efficient & value based service for our customers.

Our relationships with some of the best manufacturing companies allows us to provide the most value & meet quality expectations.
